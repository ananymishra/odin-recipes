Taco

Description

Ingredients

  • Mexican Refriend Beans
  • Tomato Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Taco shells of tortillas
  • Grated Cheese

Directions

  1. Make Refriend Beans
  2. Prepare taco shells
  3. Add refried beans to taco shell
  4. Add sour cream
  5. Top with some grated cheese
  6. Viola!
